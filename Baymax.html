About Baymax

In Big Hero 6, Baymax is voiced by Scott Adsit. In the film, he is depicted as an inflatable robot with a carbon fiber skeleton built by Hiro Hamada's older brother Tadashi to serve as a personal healthcare provider companion.

Characteristics about Baymax

  1. He's like a big Marshmallow.
  2. He's soft like a Balloon.
  3. Has a chip inside him that has the ability to reprogram him.
  4. He can learn lots of thing just by inserting those information to hi chip
